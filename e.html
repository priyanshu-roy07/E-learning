About Us

Welcome to CHYJU'S, your number one source for all things. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of all, with a focus on quality content, interactive learning, and student satisfaction.

Founded in 2024 by Shri Bill Gates Ji, CHYJU'S has come a long way from its beginnings in Phulera Village near Fakauli Bazar,Ballia,Uttar Pradesh. When Shri Bill Gates Ji first started out, his passion for Learning drove them to CHYJU'S, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into a booming online platform. We now serve students all over the world and are thrilled to be a part of the Learning platform.

We hope you enjoy our platform as much as we enjoy offering it to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
